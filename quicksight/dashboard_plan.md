# Dashboard Plan

1. **Dataset**: Connect QuickSight to Athena database `retail`, table `sales_summary`.
2. **Fields**:
   - Date (as timeline)
   - Region
   - total_qty
   - total_sales
3. **Visuals**:
   - Line chart of total_sales by Date
   - Bar chart of total_qty by Region (filter by date range)
   - KPI cards: Today’s sales, month-to-date sales, etc.
4. **Filters**:
   - Date range picker
   - Region selector
5. **Access**:
   - Share Dashboard with Business Analysts & Executives
